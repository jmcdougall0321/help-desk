    2. Clandt-quake200
  2. Click host a meeting
  3. Go to schedule a meeting (enter appropriate data)

    *(If meeting is recurring, go to advanced scheduler and enter the correct dates over a week’s span of time, that way it is the same link for each date)

  4. In attendees field, enter your email only
  5. Go to audio options at the bottom of the page and select Change audio options
  6. Integrated VOlP: “yes”
  7. Teleconference: Select WebEx teleconferencing
  8. Select: Toll and uncheck “allow access to teleconference….
  9. Select : Attendees call in
  10. Select ok
  11. Select schedule meeting tab on bottom right.

 

*(retrieve email and edit appropriately with call in: # and Conf I.D: #)
