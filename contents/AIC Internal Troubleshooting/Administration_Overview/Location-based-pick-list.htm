Location Based Pick List

Job Titles Module Item

Allows the User to modify the Job Titles available within the system and assign them to
‎particular locations.

Add Job Titles 

Select Add button

Fill in required fields (red asterisk mark)

Optional Fields: Code, Active checkbox

Once finished click Save and Close

 

Assign Locations

Select desired Job Title

Select the ‘Assign Locations’ button

Highlight desired Available Locations

Click the right arrow to move them to the Selected Locations field

Click Save and Close

 

Location View:  Displays the Available Job Titles and the Job Titles that have been assigned to
‎each Location

Select the Location View button

Choose the Location desired

The Available Job Titles will populate in the left hand box and the Selected Job Titles will populate in the right hand box

By simply selecting any number of desired Job Titles and clicking on either the right or left arrow they can then be assigned the Location chosen, or unassigned from a Location

 

Departments

Allows users to customize the Picklist for Departments and assign what locations they
‎correspond to.

Add Departments 

Select Add button

Fill in required fields (red asterisk mark)

Optional Fields: Department Code

Once finished click Save and Close

Assign Locations

Select desired Department

Select the ‘Assign Locations’ button

Highlight desired Available Locations

Click the right arrow to move them to the Selected Locations field

Click Save and Close

 

Location View:

Displays the Available Departments and the Selected Departments that
‎have to be assigned to each Incident Module

Select the Location View button

Choose the Department desired

The Available Department will populate in the left hand box and the Selected Department will populate in the right hand box

By simply selecting a number of desired Departments and clicking on either the right or
‎left arrow they can then be assigned to the Department chosen, or unassigned from a
‎Department
