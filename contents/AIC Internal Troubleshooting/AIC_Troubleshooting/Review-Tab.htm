Thank you for your email. At this time, the Review and Approval process is triggering by submitting the incident through the mini tab. The reviewers need to be selected in the mini tab before the Submit for Review button can be selected, as there could potentially be different reviewers and approvers at a given location.

Since this process has a series of steps that need to be completed before the incident can be submitted to be reviewed, saving and closing the incident would not trigger the notifications as the system would not know who to send the notifications to.
